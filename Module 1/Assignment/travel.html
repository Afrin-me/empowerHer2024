Kashmir and Uttarkhand is the most beautiful place surrounded by tall mountains and green valleys. It has a famous temple that is very old and important for many people. The air is fresh and cool, and you can see colorful flowers and rivers. Many visitors come to enjoy the peaceful nature and the stunning views of the snowy peaks. It’s a magical place where you can feel close to nature and experience calmness.
